## 🛰️ About Me

<img align="right" width="280" src="./img/programmer.gif" alt="coding" />

I'm a **Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)** engineer who builds the systems that turn the internet's messiest data credential dumps, infostealer logs, breach datasets, dark-web chatter into **clean, searchable, actionable intelligence**.

- 🧑🏻‍💻 Currently a Threat Analyst at the Canadian cybersecurity company **eSentire**.
- ❤️ My focus is the full CTI lifecycle: **collection → parsing → enrichment → delivery**.
- 🏗️ I design end-to-end intelligence pipelines and the infrastructure that searches **billions** of leaked records in sub-second time.
- 🛡️ I care a lot about doing this **securely** — encrypted storage, hardened APIs, and access-controlled edges.
- 😎 Most of my repositories are private — here's the kind of work I do anyway. 👇

## 🧠 What I Build

> A capability snapshot of the threat-intelligence platform work I do day to day.

| Domain | What I do |
|---|---|
| 🎯 **Threat-Actor Monitoring** | Automated, real-time collection across messaging platforms, paste sites, and underground sources. |
| 🦠 **Infostealer & Combolist Analysis** | Parsing, normalizing, and de-duplicating stealer logs and credential dumps at massive scale. |
| 💳 **Data Breach Intelligence** | Ingesting, fingerprinting, and enriching leaked datasets into a structured catalog. |
| 🔎 **Searchable Intel Infrastructure** | Sub-second search and lookups over billions of records. |
| ⚙️ **Pipeline Orchestration** | Resilient, self-healing workers and schedulers that collect, extract, enrich, and deliver. |
| 🔐 **Secure Storage & Delivery** | Client-side encrypted archival, authenticated APIs, and access-controlled edges. |
