## About Me

I'm a Cyber Threat Intelligence professional with a Master's degree in Threat Intelligence. My main work is CTI: turning the internet's messiest data (credential dumps, infostealer logs, breach datasets, dark-web chatter) into clean, searchable, actionable intelligence.

A second area of expertise is Agentic AI, where I build LLM agents that automate parts of the threat-intelligence and detection workflow.

- Threat Analyst at the Canadian cybersecurity company **eSentire**.
- MSc in Threat Intelligence, focused on the full CTI lifecycle: collection, parsing, enrichment, and delivery.
- I build end-to-end intelligence pipelines and search infrastructure that query **billions** of leaked records in sub-second time.
- I design AI agents that reason, call tools, and produce analyst-ready output.
- I care about doing this securely: encrypted storage, hardened APIs, guard-railed agents, and access-controlled edges.
- Most of my repositories are private. Here's the kind of work I do anyway.

## What I Build

> A capability snapshot of the threat-intelligence and Agentic AI work I do day to day.

| Domain | What I do |
|---|---|
| **Threat-Actor Monitoring** | Automated, real-time collection across messaging platforms, paste sites, and underground sources. |
| **Infostealer & Combolist Analysis** | Parsing, normalizing, and de-duplicating stealer logs and credential dumps at massive scale. |
| **Data Breach Intelligence** | Ingesting, fingerprinting, and enriching leaked datasets into a structured catalog. |
| **Searchable Intel Infrastructure** | Sub-second search and lookups over billions of records. |
| **Pipeline Orchestration** | Resilient, self-healing workers and schedulers that collect, extract, enrich, and deliver. |
| **Secure Storage & Delivery** | Client-side encrypted archival, authenticated APIs, and access-controlled edges. |
| **Agentic AI Systems** | LLM agents that reason, plan, and call tools to automate threat-intel and detection workflows. |
| **MCP Tooling** | Model Context Protocol servers that safely expose data sources and actions to AI assistants. |
| **RAG & Context Engineering** | Retrieval-augmented and context-injection pipelines that ground agents in real, relevant intelligence. |
